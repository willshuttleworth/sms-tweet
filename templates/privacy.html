Privacy Policy for smstweet

Effective Date: 12/27/2024

smstweet ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when using our service, which enables users to send tweets via SMS.

Information We Collect

To provide our service, we collect and store the following data:

  • Phone Number: Used to identify and authenticate users and to facilitate SMS communication.
  • Twitter API Tokens: Authorized by you to allow us to interact with your Twitter account for posting tweets on your behalf.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely for the following purposes:

  • Authenticating and verifying your identity as a user.
  • Sending your SMS messages as tweets via Twitter using your authorized tokens.
  • Complying with legal obligations or resolving disputes, if necessary.

Data Retention

  • We retain your phone number and Twitter API tokens only as long as you use our service.
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at willshuttle21@gmail.com.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal information to third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process.
  • To prevent fraud, enforce our terms of service, or protect the security of our platform.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request details about the data we collect and process.
  • Correction: Request corrections to any inaccuracies in your data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Revoke your authorization for us to use your Twitter API tokens.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated via SMS and will take effect on the effective date specified.
